# Employee experience override

- Mood: field operations, fast scanning and low-error task completion.
- Surface: light operational workspace with dark forest navigation and strong status separation.
- Workflow: show assignment → live update → completion as an explicit sequence.
- Timeline: native buttons for expandable activities, visible focus, clear progress and guarded completion states.
- Mobile: tables transform into labeled booking cards; timeline steps scroll horizontally without hiding state.
- Feedback: loading, empty, error, saving, upload and completion states must remain explicit.
